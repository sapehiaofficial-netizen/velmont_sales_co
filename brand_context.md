# Velmont Sales Co. — Brand Context

> Single source of truth for all Velmont branding, copy, and design decisions.
> Use this file as context whenever creating pitch decks, catalogs, DMs, agreements, social posts, or any outward-facing material.

---

## 1. What Velmont Is

**One-liner:** Velmont is the outsourced B2B sales arm for India's best small D2C brands — we put great products on shelves, in cafes, and inside corporate gift boxes.

**Elevator pitch (for brand founders):**
You built a product people love. We get it into retail, hospitality, and corporate accounts across India — at zero cost and zero risk to you. We only earn when you get paid.

**Elevator pitch (for buyers — cafes, boutiques, HR/admin teams):**
We curate India's most interesting independent brands and bring them to you with wholesale margins already worked out. No catalogs to dig through, no flaky suppliers — one person, accountable, who picks up the phone.

**What Velmont is NOT:**
- Not a marketplace or aggregator app
- Not a marketing/AI/tech agency
- Not a middleman that adds markup — commission comes from the brand's wholesale margin, never the buyer's pocket

## 3. Color Theme — "Evergreen Ledger"

An elegant, money-and-growth palette built around deep green. Feels premium, established, and calm — like a well-kept ledger, not a startup gradient.

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary | **Velmont Green** | `#1F4D3A` | Logos, headers, covers, primary buttons. Deep forest green — elegant, serious, growth. |
| Secondary | **Sage Mist** | `#A8BFA5` | Backgrounds, section dividers, soft accents |
| Accent | **Brass Gold** | `#C2A256` | Highlights, key numbers, "signed/closed" moments. Use sparingly — it's the jewelry. |
| Neutral dark | **Ink Charcoal** | `#22262A` | Body text |
| Neutral light | **Ivory Paper** | `#F7F4EC` | Page/card backgrounds instead of pure white |
| Support | **Terracotta Clay** | `#B0613F` | Rare warm accent for India-craft contexts (gifting catalogs, festive decks) |

**Rules:**
- Velmont Green + Ivory Paper is the default pairing. Gold appears in small doses only.
- Never use neon, gradients, or pure black/white. The palette should feel printed, not digital.
- Festive/Diwali materials: lean on Brass Gold + Terracotta over Velmont Green.

## 5. Brand Voice

**Voice in three words: Direct. Warm. Sharp.**

Velmont sounds like a well-read salesperson who respects everyone's time — confident without bragging, friendly without being casual-sloppy.

### Principles
1. **Lead with their gain, not our pitch.** Every message opens with what the founder or buyer gets.
2. **Numbers over adjectives.** "12% commission, paid within 7 days of your payment" beats "great rates, fast payouts."
3. **Short sentences. Plain words.** If a line works without a word, cut the word.
4. **Zero hype.** No "revolutionary," "game-changing," "synergy," "🚀". Trust is the product.
5. **Respectfully human.** First names, simple sign-offs, one clear ask per message.

### Tone by audience
- **To brand founders:** peer-to-peer, builder-to-builder. "You make it, we sell it."
- **To buyers:** service-minded and curated. "We did the homework so you don't have to."
- **In agreements/money talk:** crisp and unambiguous. No flourish where clarity matters.

### Voice examples

| ❌ Off-brand | ✅ On-brand |
|---|---|
| "We are a revolutionary sales solutions provider leveraging deep networks!" | "We get your products into stores and corporate gift boxes. You pay only when you're paid." |
| "Hi sir, hope you are doing well, I wanted to humbly request a moment of your time…" | "Hi Priya — saw your Diwali gift boxes. These would do well in corporate gifting. Free for a 10-min call this week?" |
| "Amazing margins!!" | "You buy at ₹250, sell at ₹500. We restock within a week." |

### Words we use / avoid
- **Use:** partner, curated, shelf, account, reorder, margin, accountable, handpicked
- **Avoid:** revolutionary, disrupt, hustle, leverage, synergy, solutions, "AI-powered" anything

## 6. Messaging Pillars

1. **Zero risk, aligned incentives** — Commission-only. We earn only when the brand gets paid. (Lead with this for founders.)
2. **Curated, not cluttered** — A handful of non-competing brands we actually believe in. (Lead with this for buyers.)
3. **Creative-grade materials** — Catalogs and decks most reps never produce; brands look bigger than they are.
4. **One accountable human** — A real person who picks up the phone, follows up, and owns the relationship.

**Taglines (pick per context):**
- *We sell it like it's ours.* (primary)
- *Great products, finally on shelves.*
- *India's small brands, sold properly.*

## 7. Visual & Layout Guidelines

- **Layouts:** generous whitespace, one idea per slide/page, large product photography on Ivory Paper backgrounds.
- **Photography:** natural light, real textures (wood, linen, paper). No stocky handshake images.
- **Logo direction (when made):** wordmark "VELMONT" in Playfair Display, Velmont Green, with "SALES CO." in small Inter letterspaced caps beneath. A simple monogram "V" inside a thin-line oval for avatars.
- **Documents:** every catalog/agreement footer carries: Velmont Sales Co. · name · phone · email in Ink Charcoal, 8–9pt.
